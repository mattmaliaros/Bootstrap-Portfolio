About me

Hi my name is Matt Maliaros. I am aspiring to be a full-stack developer at Rutgers.

I have a degree in Computer Information Systems from DeVry University. While I have a background in programming, I never truly mastered web development (HTML, CSS, JS, etc) until I started taking this class. I look forward to expanding my skills within this class to start freelancing, working on personal projects, and hopefully acquiring a full-time position within web development.

Here we have my edited portfolio with projects that I have been working on. These projects display my abilities learned from Rutgers and self-teachings. I will continually refine the projects as I learn more and more about these languages.
